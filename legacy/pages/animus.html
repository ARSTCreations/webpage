Staticism

The princple and art to not move from the plane of current state for all of the eternity. To be stuck in the moment, to not remember any, always fresh and new. Staticism Being in static made one in the state of equilibrium, engulfed in it's own presumable godly abillity, not remembering what it have encountered before thus not evolving and self proclaiming its omnipotential To only being remembered but always forgetting, frozen and living in the moment, over and over.
